Prambanan Temple

Be prepared to feel awestruck while visiting this compound of ancient Hindu temples. It is located at the border between Yogyakarta and Central Java provinces, and is said to have thousands of temples scattered around the area. A perfect place to take a peek into Javanese ancient culture!

The Ijen Crater

The Ijen Crater in Banyuwangi, East Java, is truly amazing, but don't just take our word for it. Hike up the mountain and arrived at the crater before dawn, so you can witness the dancing blue flames. Linger on untill sunrise to see how incredible the view is, one that can surely be put on top of your favorite list. While you are there, please help keep clean and pristine, so you can have more fun enjoying our nature's wonder!

Celebrating The Nyepi

Wishing a serene and peaceful moment for those celebrating The Saka New Year 1940 with Nyepi Day of Silence. A sacred time to delve in solemn contemplation while cherishing the bliss of nature among loved ones, Rahajeng Rahina Nyepi.

Banyumala Waterfall

The Banyumala waterfall is situated in Wanagiri, Buleleng, Bali. It will take about two hours trip to get to this waterfall, but when you get here the picturesque scenery will definitely worth your every effort. Besides admiring the scenic view, you can also swim inside its crystal-clear blue lagoon. Can you imagine how refreshing it is?
